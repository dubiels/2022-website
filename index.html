hey, i'm karolina

It's pronounced care-oh-LEE-na, and I use she/her pronouns.

I code, run a nonprofit, and fly.

I'm a teen working to increase accessibility to aerospace education, student pilot, avid cybersecurity competitor + web designer, and Men in Black enthusiast. Welcome to my corner of the internet.



I'm in a @facebook campaign!

In June of 2021, I was published in a campaign with Facebook Poland called Meet the Future. Meet the Future was an international campaign that centered on highlighting Gen Z and their unique talents and aspirations, explaining how Meta's social media has allowed them to express themselves creatively. The campaign has since been featured on countless Polish news outlets, including Brief, Onet, Obcasy, and Admonkey.



...and a Forbes-featured podcast



Etasha Donthi, the chair of She the Change, invited me talk about my work with the Girls in Aerospace Foundation and my involvement in cybersecurity. We talked about how my upbringing and interests have led me to where I am now.

Check out what I've been up to:

Girls in Aerospace Foundation

I'm the founder of a global nonprofit centered around empowering and promoting women in aerospace and aviation. We've reached over 15,000+ young women worldwide and held webinars with NASA astronauts, workshops about space law, and more.

Talaria Summer Research

In July-Aug of 2021, I worked with Leah Sacks, a PhD student in geology and planetary science at Western University, to write a research paper on Martian, Lunar, and Plutonian impact craters.

Overlake Squadron Run Club

At my local Civil Air Patrol squadron, I founded a running community for cadets who were looking to better their running ability. In the first three weeks of the club, we've already ran over 150 miles.

AFA CyberPatriot

At my Civil Air Patrol squadron, I led our CyberPatriot competition team for the 2020-2021 season. We got to the Platinum round, with my focus being on Linux Ubuntu and Debian machine hardening.
